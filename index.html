Veg Pulao Recipe

Veg Pulao is a fragrant Indian rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices. It is easy to prepare and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamom pods
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for 20 minutes. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan or pressure cooker.
  3. Add bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom. Sauté until aromatic.
  4. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili. Sauté for 1 minute.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Add soaked rice, salt, and water. Mix gently.
  8. Cover and cook until rice is done and water is absorbed.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.
Tip: For extra flavor, you can add a few mint leaves or a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.
